About Me

Hello, my name is Taylor. I'm a tractor trailer driver at FedEx Express. I'm currently residing in Washinton, DC with my amazing fiancé. At the moment, I'm completing a nanodegree with Udacity on FrontEnd Web Development.

Learning

Through my discovery of finding my love for programming, I have also discovered many challenges. With the challenges, however, comes the joy with overcoming the trials in front of you. To me, that is one of the greatest things I have learned. Continuing to push yourself and never giving up, even when it feels like you are not understading or not making progress, was one of the most important things I have learned in my life. One thing that always seemed to haunt me was constantly comparing my work to work I see online. Understanding that I only need to get better than I was yesterday is what keeps me going.

Beginnings

I first started my learning journey learning JavaScript and Racket. I used a variety of different sources and found out many things about programming. One struggle I ran into was starting one resource and then switching to another when I would get stuck. It works but progress stalls immensely. I would recommend anyone in my position to look elsewhere for different if stuck, but try to keep working through the same program all the way to the end.

Goals

My goal is to transisiton careers from truck driver to developer. I have finally found the passion and courage needed to strive for this goal. I made a promise to myself that I will not stop working toward this no matter the difficulties that lie ahead. If you've read all this way through, I want to say thank you for giving me the time and I wish you good luck in your current endeavours! Take care!
